What is the Diet Coke Oracle System?

This system is not exactly just a magic 8-ball, and it cannot predict the future. It is a structural refinery that borrows ancient logic of the I Ching to force a change in your perspective.

The Oracle works by making you strip away the emotional noise from your problems so you can look at them as pure, mechanical systems.

Privacy / How it was made

Diet Coke is built using a quite large 31B parameter opensource LLM (Gemma4), that has been pretrained using LoRA on ~70000 initial question / answer pairs (synthetically derived from ~700 real hand written pairs) and prompting templates borrowing from both The Church of Conceptual Art, and a number of our favorite English translations / versions of the I Ching.

How the Phases Work

Mode 1: The Refinery
The AI acts as a hostile editor to refine what your real conflict is. It is explicitly trained to reject vague complaints, emotional venting, or questions looking for a specific outcome. If you ask, "Will I be happy?" or "Why is my business failing?", it will block you. It demands that you reframe your problem into a precise, structural logic-gate before it will allow you to proceed.

Mode 2: The Casting
Once you achieve structural clarity, you cast the lines. You will answer a series of fixed binary questions about the tension of your situation (e.g., Assert vs. Submit, Internal vs. External). Your choices, combined with probability, generate a specific hexagram.

Mode 3: The Interpretation
The Oracle maps the Wilhelm translation of the I Ching directly onto the clinical, refined parameters you established in Phase 1, at the time of the output.

How to Get the Best Results

To get past the Refinery and get a highly accurate interpretation, you must play by the Oracle's rules:

  • Bring a specific target: Do not ask about "life." Bring a specific contract, a specific argument, or a specific creative bottleneck.
  • Be clinical: Remove your feelings. Describe the situation using structural terms like "friction," "leverage," "architecture," or "metrics."
  • Accept the pushback: The AI's rejections are part of the process. Use its prompts to narrow down exactly what is functionally broken or misaligned in your situation.

Export your final result (the full conversation and outcome) as a PDF, or just the Phase 2/3 revelation as an image you can share on social media.
