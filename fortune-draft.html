FORTUNE

a silent performance

Annabel Daou

Present your palm.

Receive your fortune.

Annabel Daou offers her services as a fortune teller. Members of the public are invited into an intimate, wordless exchange: a palm presented, a line read, a fortune written and returned — a line of text for a line in your hand.

The reading is entirely silent. No verbal hints. No theatrics. Each fortune is particular to its recipient — the questions, the writing, the exchange itself.

The piece explores trust, intimacy, and the power dynamics of transaction — what it means to open your hands to a stranger and leave with something written there. Fortune-telling is technically illegal in New York City. It remains ubiquitous.

Artist

Annabel Daou was born and raised in Beirut and lives in New York. Her intricate works probe the tensions between permanence and impermanence, belonging and dislocation. Possibilities of Repair, a monograph of her work, was published by Distanz Publishing Berlin in 2025.
